How to Use Supper Generator

Step 1: Create an account

Click the 'Sign Up' button

Enter your e-mail and password

Click the register button

Sign in: Enter your e-mail and password


Step 2: Add Your Suppers

Click add to add suppers from the suggested suppers list

Enter your own suppers and ingedients

To view your suppers: select 'View My Suppers' from the header


Step 3: Create Menu

Select the number of suppers that you want

Click the 'Pick Suppers' button

View and print your menu and shopping list

Click checkboxes to add items to your shopping list
